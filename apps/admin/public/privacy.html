M-Verify M-Pesa payment verification

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 July 2026

M-Verify is a business payment-verification service used by authorized staff to confirm M-Pesa payments, initiate STK Push requests, and review business payment activity. This policy explains how M-Verify collects, uses, stores, and shares information across its mobile, desktop, web, and server services.

Information we collect

  • Account information: username, full name, business affiliation, staff role, permissions, account status, and login timestamps.
  • M-Pesa payment information: payer phone number, customer name when supplied by Safaricom, transaction code, amount, reference, payment channel, payment time, and verification status.
  • STK Push information: customer phone number, requested amount, optional reference, Safaricom request identifiers, and the request result.
  • Verification and audit information: the staff member performing a verification, submitted lookup details, result, date and time, IP address, and user-agent information.
  • Device and session information: a generated device identifier, optional device name, session records, and security-token metadata. Authentication tokens and the device identifier may be stored locally on the device to keep authorized users signed in.

M-Verify does not request, collect, or store a customer's M-Pesa PIN.

How we use information

  • Authenticate authorized business users and enforce staff permissions.
  • Receive and display confirmed M-Pesa transactions for the correct business.
  • Initiate STK Push requests and monitor their completion, cancellation, failure, or timeout.
  • Prevent duplicate verification and maintain an audit trail of verification activity.
  • Provide business dashboards, transaction reports, staff sales records, and operational support.
  • Protect the service against misuse, investigate errors, and maintain service reliability.

How information is shared

Information may be shared only as needed with:

  • The business that owns the M-Verify account and its authorized administrators or staff, according to assigned permissions.
  • Safaricom and its M-Pesa/Daraja services when processing callbacks, payment confirmations, or STK Push requests.
  • Infrastructure and hosting providers that process data on our behalf to operate the service.
  • Regulators, law-enforcement agencies, courts, or other parties where disclosure is legally required or necessary to protect rights and security.

We do not sell personal information and do not use personal information for third-party advertising.

Data security

M-Verify uses HTTPS, password hashing, encrypted storage for configured M-Pesa API credentials, role-based access controls, masked phone numbers in ordinary responses, rate limiting, session controls, and audit logs. No internet service can guarantee absolute security, but we maintain safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information processed.

Data retention

Account, payment, STK Push, and verification records are retained for as long as necessary to provide the service, support business reconciliation and audit requirements, prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and meet applicable legal obligations. Sessions may be revoked by an administrator or removed when no longer needed.

Your choices and rights

Depending on applicable law, individuals may request access to, correction of, restriction of, or deletion of their personal information. Staff users should first contact their business administrator. Customers and other individuals may contact M-Verify using the address below. Some payment or audit records may need to be retained where required for legitimate business, security, or legal purposes.

Children

M-Verify is a business tool intended for authorized workers and business administrators. It is not directed to children and is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age.

International processing

Service providers may process information in countries other than the country where it was collected. Where required, we use appropriate contractual, organizational, and technical safeguards for such processing.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the service, legal requirements, or data practices change. The effective date above will be updated when a revised policy is published.
